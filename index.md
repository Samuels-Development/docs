## FAQ

<div class="faq-list">
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Where can I get support?</summary>
    <p>Join our <a href="https://discord.gg/Tu94MCDDEa" target="_blank">Discord server</a> and open a support ticket. Our team typically responds within a few hours.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Which frameworks are supported?</summary>
    <p>All scripts support <strong>QBCore</strong>, <strong>QBox</strong>, and <strong>ESX</strong> with automatic detection. You do not need to change any settings for framework compatibility.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Do I need ox_lib?</summary>
    <p>Most scripts require <code>ox_lib</code> as a dependency. Check the installation page of each resource for its specific dependency list.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>How do I update a script?</summary>
    <p>Download the latest version from <a href="https://keymaster.fivem.net" target="_blank">Keymaster</a>, replace the files in your <code>resources</code> folder, and restart the resource. Database migrations run automatically.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Where do I buy your scripts?</summary>
    <p>All scripts are sold <strong>exclusively</strong> on <a href="https://fivem.samueldev.shop" target="_blank">fivem.samueldev.shop</a>. Do not purchase from unauthorized resellers.</p>
  </details>
</div>

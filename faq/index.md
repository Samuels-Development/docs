# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about our scripts.

## General

### Where can I buy your scripts?

All scripts are sold exclusively on [fivem.samueldev.shop](https://fivem.samueldev.shop). Do not purchase from any other source — unauthorized resellers may distribute tampered or outdated versions.

### How do I get support?

Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/FzPehMQaBQ) and open a support ticket. We offer assistance for all of our scripts.

### What frameworks are supported?

All scripts support **QBCore** (`qb-core` / `qbx_core`) and **ESX** (`es_extended`). Framework detection is automatic — just drop the resource into your server.

### What inventory systems are supported?

We primarily support **ox_inventory**, **qb-inventory**, and **es_extended** default inventory. Some features (like blueprints, metadata, stashes) require **ox_inventory**.

### Do I need to set up the database manually?

No. All scripts handle database table creation automatically on first startup via `oxmysql`.

### What targeting systems are supported?

All scripts work with **ox_target**, **qb-target**, and **qtarget**.
